Profile

About

My background sits between education, design research, and applied systems thinking. I am particularly interested in how human-centred approaches can support complex real-world settings where coordination, decision-making, and user experience intersect.

What I work on

  • Healthcare service and workflow improvement
  • Educational technology and learning experience design
  • Data and knowledge visualisation for sensemaking
  • Interactive prototypes for communication and testing

How I work

  • Grounding design decisions in field insights and stakeholder needs
  • Translating qualitative and quantitative findings into clear interventions
  • Using visual and interactive artefacts to support alignment and iteration
  • Balancing research rigor with practical implementation constraints
